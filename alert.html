Disaster Alert Settings

The Disaster Alert Settings panel provides a centralized, user-friendly interface designed to keep individuals informed and safe during emergencies. It allows users to customize exactly how they receive critical, real-time emergency situations updates. The portal offers two primary channels for receiving these notifications, ensuring maximum coverage and accessibility:

Email Alerts: Users can input their email addresses to receive comprehensive, written updates regarding ongoing disasters, safety protocols, and evacuation notices directly in their inbox.

SMS Alerts: For more immediate, on-the-go notifications, users can link their phone numbers. This ensures that time-sensitive emergency alerts reach their mobile devices instantly, even without an active internet connection.

Once the contact information is filled out, users can click the Activate Alert button to save their preferences and seamlessly enroll in the emergency broadcasting network.
