DeSuung (Guardians of Peace)

Main Volunteer Division

The main volunteer divisions of DeSuung include disaster response teams, community service volunteers, health and safety support teams, traffic management volunteers, and emergency relief squads. These volunteer divisions help during disasters, public events, emergencies, and national service activities across Bhutan.

About Leadership

Administered via the Central Command Office. Regulates large-scale civic support, volunteer assignments, and community integration plans.

Rescue

The DeSuung plays an important role in rescuing and helping people during emergencies and natural disasters in Bhutan. DeSuups assist during floods, landslides, fires, road accidents, and health emergencies by supporting rescue operations, providing relief materials, managing evacuation, and helping local communities. Their dedication, discipline, and volunteer service have helped save many lives across the country.

Record

The DeSuung has a strong record of helping people during disasters, emergencies, and national crises in Bhutan. DeSuups have assisted in flood and landslide rescue operations, disaster relief distribution, road safety management, and community support programs. During the COVID-19 pandemic, they played a major role in quarantine monitoring, delivery of essential supplies, and public safety services, earning great respect for their dedication and volunteer service to the nation.
