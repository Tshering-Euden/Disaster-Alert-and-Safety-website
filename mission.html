Mission

Our mission is to provide fast disaster alerts, emergency response services, safety awareness, and preparedness guidance to protect people during natural and man-made disasters.


Deconstructing Our Purpose: Action, Response, and Impact
At the core of the Disaster Alert and Safety Portal is a singular, unwavering mandate: to save lives, reduce human suffering, and fortify our nation's communities by bridging the gap between immediate vulnerability and decisive action.
When a crisis strikes—whether a sudden natural hazard or an unexpected man-made emergency seconds determine outcomes. Our mission is built on four core operational pillars designed to provide stability, speed, and safety when people need it most.




    Pillar 1: Fast & Precision-Targeted Disaster Alerts


    A disaster alert is only effective if it reaches the right people in time to make a difference. We leverage modern communication technologies to bypass traditional bottlenecks and deliver life-saving data directly to the public.


  • Localized Early Warning Systems:Integrating real-time geological, meteorological, and municipal data feeds to detect escalating hazards early, providing citizens with crucial minutes to evacuate or shelter in place.

  • Omnichannel Broadcasting Networks:Ensuring notifications are never missed. Our infrastructure is engineered to deliver instantaneous updates simultaneously across mobile app push notifications, widespread SMS networks, and live web alerts.

  • Dynamic Threat Tracking:Providing ongoing, real-time adjustments as a situation evolves, ensuring communities know exactly when a threat passes or if an active hazard has shifted direction.


    • Pillar 2: Unified Emergency Response Coordination


    During a crisis, confusion is a major threat. Our platform serves as a definitive, centralized digital gateway that instantly bridges the gap between citizens and frontline emergency personnel, removing critical friction from emergency communications.


  • Emergency Medical Services (112): Facilitating immediate routing for medical trauma, injuries, and ambulance dispatch during mass-casualty events or individual crises.

  • Fire & Rescue Services (110):Streamlining reporting lines for urban fires, structural collapses, forest fires, and specialized rescue operations.

  • Royal Bhutan Police (113): Ensuring rapid law enforcement deployment to manage public safety, secure compromised areas, and coordinate traffic control during mass evacuations.

  • Desuung - Guardians of Peace (1414):Activating a powerful citizen volunteer network to scale up search-and-rescue operations, manage disaster relief camps, and distribute critical supplies to affected regions.


    • Pillar 3: Comprehensive Safety Awareness


    True disaster management begins long before an alert is triggered. We are committed to shifting the public mindset from passive reaction to active preparedness through accessible, high-quality safety education.


  • Actionable Survival Guides: Creating step-by-step instructions customized for regional threats—such as earthquakes, flash floods, landslides, and structural fires—detailing exact behaviors to adopt during an event.

  • Interactive Digital Resources: Providing multimedia content, clear infographics, and easily digestible safety checklists that make disaster education engaging and easy to remember for citizens of all ages.


    • Pillar 4: Inclusivity & Preparedness Guidance


    We believe that safety is a universal right. Our mission ensures that no community is left behind, regardless of geographical isolation or economic vulnerability.


  • Leaving No One Behind: Designing a digitally accessible platform that ensures warning networks reach remote, high-risk rural areas and vulnerable populations seamlessly.

  • Community Drill Kits & Evacuation Frameworks:Mapping out local safe zones, emergency shelters, and clear routes while supplying schools, workplaces, and neighborhoods with the resources needed to conduct effective safety drills.
